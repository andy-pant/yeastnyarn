  • Early 2000s

    A Knitter is Born

    Andrea's grandma teachers her how to crochet, and eventually knit.

    A Baker is Born

    Making rice krispy squares and lemon loaf with her grandma, blueberry muffins with her mom, are some of Andrea's fondest memories as a kid.

  • December 2017

    Hang up the cleats, pick up the needles

    No longer able to play rugby, Andrea orders a knitting kit as an attempt at relaxing during a stressful time at work.

  • 2018 - 2020

    Tangle me up like grandma's yarn

    Knitting takes over Andrea's life (and living room). Starting with babywear for her many pregnant friends, she tries making toys too and eventually gets back into crochet as it's easier for toymaking.

  • March 2020

    Lockdown

    Covid-19 pandemic starts sweeping through the world. New Zealand goes into lockdown. More knitting and crochet occurs. Thoughts of starting an etsy store creep in.

    A Starter is Born

    Stuck at home with little to do outside of work hours, Andrea jumps on the sourdough bandwagon. "Henrietta" - her hearty sourdough starter - is born.

  • April - October 2020

    The New Normal
