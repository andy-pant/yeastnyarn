The Yeast n' Yarn Evolution Timeline

Date Noteable Activity
Early 2000s Andrea's grandma teachers how to crochet, and eventually knit.
December 2017 No longer able to play rugby, Andrea orders a knitting kit as an attempt at relaxing during a stressful time at work.
2018 - 2020 Knitting takes over Andrea's life (and living room). Starting with babywear for her many pregnant friends, she tries making toys too and eventually gets back into crochet as it's easier for toymaking.
March 2020 Covid-19 pandemic starts sweeping through the world. New Zealand goes into lockdown. More knitting and crochet occurs. Thoughts of starting an etsy store creep in.
End of March 2020 Stuck at home with little to do outside of work hours, Andrea jumps on the sourdough bandwagon. "Henrietta" - her hearty sourdough starter - is born.
April - October 2020 Baking all the time. Mostly sourdough, but other baking too. Many lockdown / "post Covid" feelings are being eaten.
October 2020 The idea to combine Andrea's love of knitting and baking into a wonderful cafe called Yeast n' Yarn is born! Courtesy of Bryce.
November 2020 Wanting to improve her web development skills for her duties managing the Royal Aeronautical Society's New Zealand website, Andrea starts up an online Udemy web development course. The bones of this website are born.
